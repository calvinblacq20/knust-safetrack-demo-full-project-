Every feature designed around real campus safety scenarios — from late-night library sessions to emergency response.

SOS Emergency Alert
Hold the SOS button for 2 seconds to instantly alert campus security with your live GPS location. A progress ring shows activation status.
INSTANT RESPONSE
Walk With Me
Request a security escort or walk with fellow students. Live location is shared throughout. Arrive safely — your companion is notified the moment you do.

Live Campus Map
Real-time map of KNUST campus with security patrol locations, active walk routes, SOS hotspots, and a traffic heatmap for security personnel.
REAL-TIME
SafeTrack AI
AI-powered campus assistant. Ask about safe routes, report issues, get safety tips, or find the nearest help point — powered by Claude AI with KNUST-specific knowledge.

Security Dashboard
Full command center for security personnel — live SOS alerts, patrol management, active walk monitoring, broadcast alerts, and incident heatmaps.

HOW IT
WORKS

01
Sign In with your KNUST credentials
Students use their KNUST student email. Security personnel use their staff ID. Role-based access ensures the right tools for the right people.
02
Your live map loads instantly
See active security patrols, safe walk routes, and campus alerts on an interactive map centered on KNUST. Your location is shown in real time.
03
Request help or start a walk
Use Walk With Me to find a companion, share your location, or hold SOS to alert security. Everything is one or two taps away.
04
Arrive safely. Confirm & close.
Tap "I Arrived Safely" to notify your companion and security. Your trip is logged automatically for your safety history.
